About Us:

Welcome to our website, where compassion and innovation come together to create a brighter future for our feline friends. Our journey began with a simple act of kindness - feeding the neighborhood cats. What started as a small gesture soon grew into a deep commitment to improving the lives of these beloved creatures.

Driven by our passion, we took it upon ourselves to build a shelter, providing a safe haven for homeless cats in our community. We knew that to make a lasting impact, we had to tackle the root of the issue. That's when we discovered Trap-Neuter-Return (TNR), a humane method to manage the feral cat population. Tirelessly, we worked to spay and neuter the cats, ensuring their health and preventing the cycle of overpopulation.

Then came a turning point in our lives - the COVID-19 pandemic. Amidst the challenges and uncertainties, we found an opportunity for personal growth and self-discovery. We embarked on a coding bootcamp, eager to expand our skill set and find new ways to address global issues.

Now, armed with our newfound knowledge and fueled by our passion, we are leveraging technology to make an even greater impact. Through this app/website we are developing, we aim to tackle a world issue that affects both cats and humans alike. Our innovative solution combines the power of data, community engagement, and collaboration to create positive change on a global scale.

We invite you to join us on this exciting journey. Together, we can transform the lives of cats in need and create a brighter future for all. Let's make a difference, one paw at a time.
