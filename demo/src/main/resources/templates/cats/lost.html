Lost Cat: What to Do

  • Search your home and surrounding area
  • Post flyers
  • Contact local shelters and rescue organizations

Search your home and surrounding area

The first thing you should do is search your home and the immediate area around it. Check all rooms, closets, and hiding spots. Call out your cat's name and listen for any response.

Post flyers

Create a flyer with your cat's photo, name, and a description of their appearance. Include your contact information and the date and location your cat went missing. Post the flyers around your neighborhood, local parks, and community centers.

Contact local shelters and rescue organizations

Call or visit local animal shelters, rescue organizations, and veterinary clinics to see if your cat has been brought in. Provide them with your cat's description and your contact information. You can also post information about your lost cat on social media and online pet directories.
